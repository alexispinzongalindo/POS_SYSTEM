Welcome to IslaPOS Training.
This training covers the full app from setup to daily operations.
Start with Login and Setup: sign in, verify your business information, and confirm taxes.
In Admin Overview, you manage locations, users, permissions, and system settings.
In Restaurants and Branches, configure each location and keep operations separated.
In Menu and Products, build categories, prices, and modifiers before taking orders.
In POS Order Flow, open a ticket, add items, adjust quantities, and complete payment.
In Customers and Receipts, attach name, email, and phone for lookup and email receipts.
In Tables and Floor Plan, configure sections, seat capacity, and service layout.
In Live Table Service, monitor active tables and reopen checks quickly.
In Kitchen Display System, send tickets to prep stations and track status in real time.
In Reports and Food Cost, review sales, margins, and performance daily.
In Inventory and Recipes, track stock movement, purchases, and ingredient usage.
In Staff, Time Clock, and Payroll, control labor, shifts, and payroll review.
In Integrations and Delivery, connect channels and verify status before service.
In Support Station, create, update, and close support cases with resolution notes.
Use the Training Library to onboard new staff and keep procedures consistent.
Repeat this training regularly to maintain fast, accurate operations.
