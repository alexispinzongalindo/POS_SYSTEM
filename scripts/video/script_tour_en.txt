Welcome to IslaPOS, the modern point-of-sale system designed for restaurants and cafes.

In this tour, we'll explore the key features that make IslaPOS simple, fast, and reliable.

First, let's look at the intuitive menu interface. Browse categories like appetizers, main courses, beverages, and desserts with ease.

Adding items to an order is as simple as tapping the item. Modify quantities, add special instructions, and see your order total update in real-time.

The order summary keeps everything organized. Review items, adjust quantities, or remove items before finalizing.

When you're ready to checkout, multiple payment options are available: cash, card, or digital payments.

IslaPOS also includes powerful reporting features. Track sales, monitor inventory, and analyze customer preferences.

The system works offline, ensuring your business never stops, even without internet connectivity.

Thank you for exploring IslaPOS. Let's transform your point-of-sale experience together.
