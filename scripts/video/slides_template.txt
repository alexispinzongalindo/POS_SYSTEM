## Slide Design Guidelines

### Visual Consistency
- Use brand colors consistently
- Maintain same font family throughout
- Keep layout template uniform

### Content Guidelines
- Limit text to 2-3 bullet points per slide
- Use high-quality images (1920x1080)
- Ensure good contrast for readability

### Image Requirements
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (16:9 aspect ratio)
- Format: PNG or JPG
- File naming: slide_001.png, slide_002.png, etc.
- Location: public/slides/tour/ or public/slides/training/

### Tips
- Keep slides simple and focused
- Use screenshots from actual application
- Add arrows or highlights to guide attention
- Test readability on different screen sizes
